Snapchat effects offer a delightful way to enhance your photos and videos on the app. Using a wide range of filters to choose from, you can transform your selfies into works of art or add a touch of humor to your snaps. If you desire to look like a cute puppy, wear a flower crown, or try out a funky pair of sunglasses, Snapchat filters have got you covered.
